Features

Everything You Need to Stay Connected

From finding events to creating signals, SPOT has all the tools to help you connect with your campus community.

Interactive Map

See events happening around you on a beautiful interactive map with real-time zone activity.

Create Signals

Drop a signal to let others know you are gathering at a location. Perfect for study groups.

Hashtag Feeds

Subscribe to hashtags that interest you and get personalized feeds of posts.

Event Discovery

Browse university and student organization events. Filter by category, location, or host type.

Event Chat

Chat with other attendees before and during events to coordinate and connect.

Real-time Notifications

Get notified about new events, signal activity, and posts in your subscribed hashtags.
